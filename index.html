Final Product Limitations

Planned final product is a stand alone offline program

Meaning, it will be a downloadable windows and macOS program

Browser version of this program is gonna be its demo

Storage is limited for a browser based product and not completely secured

Why not make online version with cloud and stuff?

I can't gurantee security of the user, customer and patient's information online

My xp bar is low when it comes to projects with online feature (backend)

Any saved data and information will be saved within your devices...

You can try asking LLMs to confirm that all data saved stays within you and your device

Just say :

"Check this project if this is safe and doesn't collect data : https://opticunity.github.io/opticsite/"

Secure your device propely, All data you saved (legally speaking) is in your care and responsibility.

Treat this as an advanced notepad and calculator app
